Our Services

Breakbulk Cargo

  • Our Project Logistics teams focus on planning, managing, and undertaking special logistics projects of any scale.
  • We work closely with engineering and contractor customers to ensure projects are completed on time and to specifications.
  • We coordinate the flow of materials and handle restrictions in difficult or remote areas.
  • We manage site operations, provide flexible options, and stage supply lines to meet strict deadlines.

Container Trading

  • We supply standard containers delivered straight from the factory to your preferred location.
  • We maintain stocks of one-trip containers across ports.
  • We provide a wide range of shipping and storage solutions.
  • We offer both new and used containers for major project logistics and storage needs.

Customs Clearance

  • We ensure compliance with all laws and regulations governing import and export.
  • Our customs compliance program guarantees accurate documentation.
  • We liaise with relevant agencies to prevent delays in customs procedures.
  • We efficiently manage procedural works to avoid cost overruns for clients.

Freight Forwarding

  • We handle all types of cargo with no restrictions on size, weight, location, or transport mode.
  • Our operations team provides full shipment visibility anytime and anywhere.
  • We offer comprehensive sea freight services, including project cargo and heavy lifting.
  • We optimize logistics chains for efficient transport management.

Inland Transport

  • We ensure maximum efficiency and safety while handling all types of cargo.
  • Empty Container Transportation: We are pioneers in moving empty containers at nominal rates across major ports.
  • Export and Import Containers: We transport 20ft, 40ft, and 45ft containers to and from major ports.

Linear Agency

  • We coordinate equipment movement with vessel operators and container carriers.
  • Our inventory includes dry and special equipment categories.
